How to make a flat white coffee

When you want to start to work with energy and your mind needs to be alert then you have to drink a delicious and strong coffee !!!

Ingredients

  • Coffee Maker Machine
  • Expresso Capsule
  • Water
  • Whole Milk

Preparation

  1. Fill the water repository of the Coffee Machine
  2. Turn on the Coffee Maker Machine
  3. Place the Expresso Coffee Capsule in the CoffeeMaker Machine
  4. Start the make the Coffee as Expresso
  5. Add milk to taste(optional you can heat themilk before)
  6. Drink...
